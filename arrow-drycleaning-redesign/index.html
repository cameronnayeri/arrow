Our Story

Four decades of care in Harvard Square

Arrow Dry Cleaners opened on Massachusetts Avenue in 1984. What began as a neighborhood dry cleaner grew into an institution — recognized by the City of Cambridge, featured on Boston news, and trusted by generations of Harvard and MIT community members.

Owner Mahmood Mojahed and his team believe in something simple: treat every garment as if it were your own. That philosophy has earned the loyalty of customers who have been coming back for 15, 20, even 30 years.
