Our History

Rooted in Harvard Square since 1984

Arrow Dry Cleaners opened on Massachusetts Avenue in 1984, nestled in the heart of one of America’s most intellectually vibrant neighborhoods. From the very beginning, the focus was simple: treat every customer’s garments as carefully as you’d treat your own.

Over four decades, the neighborhood has changed around us — but our commitment to quality and personal service has remained constant. We’ve pressed the suits of professors, preserved the wedding gowns of students who came back as parents, and stored the winter coats of countless academics heading home for the summer.

Owner Mahmood Mojahed has built the business on something that no chain can replicate: genuine relationships. Customers who have been coming here for 15, 20, even 30 years aren’t just loyal — they’re family. We know their names, their preferences, and the stories behind the garments they bring us.

In 2022, the City of Cambridge officially recognized Arrow Dry Cleaners as a Cambridge Legacy Business — an acknowledgment of our lasting cultural and community contribution to this neighborhood we’ve called home for over 40 years.

What We Stand For

Our commitments to you

These aren’t marketing statements — they’re the principles we’ve operated by for over four decades.

Consistent Quality

Whether it’s your everyday work shirt or a once-in-a-lifetime gown, every item receives the same careful attention. No shortcuts, no exceptions.

Personal Service

We remember your name and your preferences. This isn’t a drop-box operation — it’s a relationship. We take that seriously.

Environmental Responsibility

We use eco-conscious cleaning processes and work to minimize our environmental footprint. Good for your garments, good for Cambridge.

Fair, Honest Pricing

Premium quality in Harvard Square doesn’t have to mean premium prices. We’ve always believed excellent garment care should be accessible.

Community Commitment

From free summer storage for students to staying open through difficult times, we’ve always shown up for this community.

Satisfaction Guaranteed

If something isn’t right, we’ll make it right. A promise we’ve kept for 40 years and one we intend to keep for 40 more.

Student & Academic Community

Serving Harvard & MIT since day one

Arrow has been a trusted resource for the university community since 1984. We understand the rhythm of academic life — the conference presentations that need a perfectly pressed suit, the end-of-semester rush, the summer break.

That’s why we offer complimentary summer garment storage for students and academics. Leave your winter wardrobe with us when you head home, and it’ll be cleaned, stored safely, and ready when you return in the fall.
